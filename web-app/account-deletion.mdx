title: "Account Deletion"
description: "How to permanently delete your LaserSell account and what happens to your data."
---

You can permanently delete your LaserSell account at any time from the Settings page. Account deletion is immediate and irreversible.

## How to Delete Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** from the sidebar in your [LaserSell dashboard](https://app.lasersell.io/settings).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to Delete Account">
    At the bottom of the Settings page, find the **Delete Account** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Click **Delete Account**, then type `DELETE` in the confirmation field to confirm. This step prevents accidental deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your LaserSell account and profile
- All wallets and wallet keys
- Trading sessions and trade history
- Analytics, insights, and achievements
- API keys
- Referral profile, referral relationships, and referral earnings
- Push notification subscriptions
- Billing and subscription data

<Warning>
  **Unclaimed SOL referral earnings are forfeited.** If you have claimable SOL earnings, claim them before deleting your account. Once deleted, earnings cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

## What Is NOT Deleted

- **Whop membership**: If you have an active Whop subscription, it is not automatically canceled. Cancel your subscription on [Whop](https://whop.com/billing/) before deleting your LaserSell account to avoid being charged.
- **On-chain transactions**: Any transactions already submitted to the Solana blockchain are permanent and cannot be reversed.

## After Deletion

- You will be signed out immediately
- You can create a new account with the same email address, but no data will be restored
- If you have an active Whop subscription, cancel it separately at [whop.com/billing](https://whop.com/billing/)
