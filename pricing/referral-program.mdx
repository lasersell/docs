Invite traders to LaserSell and earn a share of their SOL trading fees. Build a referral chain up to three levels deep, and earn subscription commissions when your referrals upgrade to a paid plan. Trading fee earnings apply to SOL trades only; USD1 trades are excluded.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Program">
    Sign in to your [LaserSell dashboard](https://app.lasersell.io/referrals) and claim a unique referral username. Your link becomes `lasersell.io/@you`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share Your Link">
    Send your referral link to friends, communities, or your audience. When someone signs up through your link, they are permanently linked to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Rewards">
    Earn a percentage of your referrals' SOL trading fees, plus subscription commissions in USD. Rewards are claimable after a 7 day hold period. USD1 trades are not eligible for trading fee earnings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Trading Fee Referrals

Every time one of your referrals executes a SOL trade through the LaserSell router, you earn a percentage of the protocol fee that LaserSell collects. Earnings extend up to three levels deep in your referral chain. USD1 trades are excluded from referral trading fee earnings.

| Tier | Relationship | Your Earning |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Tier 1** | You refer Alice (direct) | **30%** of Alice's trading fee |
| **Tier 2** | Alice refers Bob | **3%** of Bob's trading fee |
| **Tier 3** | Bob refers Carol | **2%** of Carol's trading fee |

<Accordion title="Example calculation">
A free tier user pays a 2% trading fee on a 1 SOL sell. That is 0.02 SOL in fees.

- **Tier 1 referrer** earns 30% of 0.02 SOL = **0.006 SOL**
- **Tier 2 referrer** earns 3% of 0.02 SOL = **0.0006 SOL**
- **Tier 3 referrer** earns 2% of 0.02 SOL = **0.0004 SOL**

Paid tier users pay lower fees (0.9% on Professional, 0.5% on Advanced), so the per trade earning is smaller, but active traders generate consistent volume.
</Accordion>

## Subscription Referrals

When a user you **directly referred** subscribes to a paid plan, you earn a **30% recurring commission** on every invoice they pay, for as long as they remain subscribed. Subscription commissions apply to direct referrals only (Tier 1). The multi tier chain does not apply to subscriptions.

| Plan | Monthly Price | Your Commission |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| Professional Edition | $99/mo | **$29.70/mo** (recurring)* |
| Advanced Exit Engine | $249/mo | **$74.70/mo** (recurring)* |

*Amounts shown are before Stripe's standard payout fees. Stripe may deduct a small fee when you withdraw to your bank account.

Subscription commissions are paid in USD through **Stripe** with a **7 day hold**. After connecting your bank account from the referral dashboard, commissions are automatically transferred to your Stripe account and paid out after the hold period.

## Payouts

### Trading fee earnings (SOL)

Trading fee earnings are paid in SOL to any Solana wallet address you provide. Only SOL-denominated trades are eligible; USD1 trades do not generate referral trading fee earnings. All SOL earnings are subject to a **7 day hold period** before becoming claimable. You can claim from the referral dashboard whenever your claimable balance exceeds **0.01 SOL**.

Payouts are sent from the LaserSell treasury wallet. The USD value of your earnings is determined by your referrals' trading volume and the fee tier they are on.

### Subscription earnings (USD)

Subscription commissions are paid in USD through Stripe with a **7 day hold**. After the hold period, funds are automatically paid out to your connected bank account.

## Earning Status Lifecycle

Each earning goes through the following stages:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending / Held** | Earnings are in the 7 day hold period. |
| **Claimable** | The hold period has passed. SOL earnings can be claimed from the dashboard. |
| **Claimed / Paid** | Paid out to your wallet (SOL) or bank account (USD). |

SOL trading fee earnings must be manually claimed from the dashboard. Subscription commissions are automatically transferred to your Stripe account after the 7 day hold and paid out to your bank.
