Invite traders to LaserSell and earn a share of their SOL trading fees. Build a referral chain up to three levels deep, and earn subscription commissions when your referrals upgrade to a paid plan. Trading fee earnings are split atomically on chain at trade time, so SOL arrives in your wallet instantly. USD1 trades are excluded.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Program">
    Sign in to your [LaserSell dashboard](https://app.lasersell.io/referrals), claim a unique referral username, and set your payout wallet (any Solana wallet address). Your link becomes `lasersell.io/@you`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share Your Link">
    Send your referral link to friends, communities, or your audience. When someone signs up through your link, they are permanently linked to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Rewards">
    Earn a percentage of your referrals' SOL trading fees, paid instantly on chain to your payout wallet the moment they trade. You also earn subscription commissions in USD through Whop. USD1 trades are not eligible for trading fee earnings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Trading Fee Referrals

Every time one of your referrals executes a SOL trade through the LaserSell router, you earn a percentage of the protocol fee that LaserSell collects. Earnings extend up to three levels deep in your referral chain. USD1 trades are excluded from referral trading fee earnings.

| Tier | Relationship | Your Earning |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Tier 1** | You refer Alice (direct) | **30%** of Alice's trading fee |
| **Tier 2** | Alice refers Bob | **3%** of Bob's trading fee |
| **Tier 3** | Bob refers Carol | **2%** of Carol's trading fee |

<Accordion title="Example calculation">
A free tier user pays a 2% trading fee on a 1 SOL sell. That is 0.02 SOL in fees.

- **Tier 1 referrer** earns 30% of 0.02 SOL = **0.006 SOL**
- **Tier 2 referrer** earns 3% of 0.02 SOL = **0.0006 SOL**
- **Tier 3 referrer** earns 2% of 0.02 SOL = **0.0004 SOL**

Paid tier users pay lower fees (0.9% on Professional, 0.5% on Advanced), so the per trade earning is smaller, but active traders generate consistent volume.
</Accordion>

## Subscription Referrals

When a user you **directly referred** subscribes to a paid plan, you earn a **30% recurring commission** on every invoice they pay, for as long as they remain subscribed. Subscription commissions apply to direct referrals only (Tier 1). The multi tier chain does not apply to subscriptions.

| Plan | Monthly Price | Your Commission |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| Professional Edition | $99/mo | **$29.70/mo** (recurring)* |
| Advanced Exit Engine | $249/mo | **$74.70/mo** (recurring)* |

*Amounts shown are before Whop's payout fees. Whop may deduct a small fee when you withdraw to your bank account.

Subscription commissions are paid in USD through **Whop** with a **30 day hold**. After connecting your payout method from the referral dashboard, commissions are automatically transferred and paid out after the hold period. Subject to Whop's payout schedule and policies.

## Payouts

### Trading fee earnings (SOL)

Trading fee earnings are paid in SOL instantly at trade time. When one of your referrals executes a trade, the protocol fee is split atomically on chain and your share is sent directly to your payout wallet in the same transaction. There is no hold period and no manual step required. Only SOL denominated trades are eligible; USD1 trades do not generate referral trading fee earnings.

You set your payout wallet when creating your referral code. You can update it at any time from the referral dashboard.

<Note>
  **Rent exemption note:** If your payout wallet is new and empty (0 SOL), very small payouts under ~0.002 SOL may be deferred until a larger trade comes through. Fund your wallet with any amount of SOL to receive all payouts.
</Note>

### Subscription earnings (USD)

Subscription commissions are paid in USD through Whop with a **30 day hold**. After the hold period, funds are automatically paid out to your connected payout method. Subject to Whop's payout schedule and policies.

## Earning Status

**Trading fee earnings (SOL)** are instant. The moment a referred user trades, your share of the fee is delivered on chain to your payout wallet. There is no pending state or manual action needed. You can verify each payout on a Solana block explorer.

**Subscription commissions (USD)** follow Whop's payout schedule. After a 30 day hold period, commissions are automatically transferred to your connected payout method through Whop.
