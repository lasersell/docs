Invite traders to LaserSell and earn a share of their trading fees in SOL. Build a referral chain up to three levels deep, and earn subscription commissions when your referrals upgrade to a paid plan.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Program">
    Sign in to your [LaserSell dashboard](https://app.lasersell.io/referrals) and claim a unique referral username. Your link becomes `lasersell.io/@you`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share Your Link">
    Send your referral link to friends, communities, or your audience. When someone signs up through your link, they are permanently linked to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Rewards">
    Earn a percentage of your referrals' trading fees in SOL, plus subscription commissions in USD. Rewards are claimable after a 30 day hold period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Trading Fee Referrals

Every time one of your referrals executes a trade through the LaserSell router, you earn a percentage of the protocol fee that LaserSell collects. Earnings extend up to three levels deep in your referral chain.

| Tier | Relationship | Your Earning |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Tier 1** | You refer Alice (direct) | **30%** of Alice's trading fee |
| **Tier 2** | Alice refers Bob | **3%** of Bob's trading fee |
| **Tier 3** | Bob refers Carol | **2%** of Carol's trading fee |

<Accordion title="Example calculation">
A free tier user pays a 2% trading fee on a 1 SOL sell. That is 0.02 SOL in fees.

- **Tier 1 referrer** earns 30% of 0.02 SOL = **0.006 SOL**
- **Tier 2 referrer** earns 3% of 0.02 SOL = **0.0006 SOL**
- **Tier 3 referrer** earns 2% of 0.02 SOL = **0.0004 SOL**

Paid tier users pay lower fees (0.9% on Professional, 0.5% on Advanced), so the per trade earning is smaller, but active traders generate consistent volume.
</Accordion>

## Subscription Referrals

When a user you **directly referred** subscribes to a paid plan, you earn a **15% recurring commission** on every invoice they pay, for as long as they remain subscribed. Subscription commissions apply to direct referrals only (Tier 1). The multi tier chain does not apply to subscriptions.

| Plan | Monthly Price | Your Commission |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| Professional Edition | $99/mo | **$14.85/mo** (recurring) |
| Advanced Exit Engine | $249/mo | **$37.35/mo** (recurring) |

Subscription commissions are paid in USD through **Stripe Connect**. Connect your bank account from the referral dashboard to receive payouts.

## Payouts

### Trading fee earnings (SOL)

Trading fee earnings are paid in SOL to any Solana wallet address you provide. You can claim your earnings from the referral dashboard whenever your claimable balance exceeds **0.01 SOL**.

Payouts are sent from the LaserSell treasury wallet. The USD value of your earnings is determined by your referrals' trading volume and the fee tier they are on.

### Subscription earnings (USD)

Subscription commissions are paid in USD through Stripe Connect. After connecting your bank account, claimable commissions are transferred automatically.

## Rules and Requirements

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Username" icon="user">
    Alphanumeric only, 3 to 15 characters. You choose it once and it cannot be changed. Certain reserved names (admin, support, etc.) are blocked.
  </Card>
  <Card title="30 Day Hold" icon="clock">
    All earnings enter a 30 day hold period before becoming claimable. This protects against chargebacks and fraud.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Volume Threshold" icon="shield">
    Your referral must accumulate at least **10 SOL** in total trading volume before your earnings from their trades unlock. This prevents self referral abuse.
  </Card>
  <Card title="One Referrer Per Account" icon="link">
    Each account can only be referred by one person. The referral link used at signup is permanent and cannot be changed.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Earning Status Lifecycle

Each earning goes through the following stages:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Locked** | Your referral has not yet reached the 10 SOL volume threshold. Earnings accrue but are not yet eligible. |
| **Pending** | Volume threshold met. The 30 day hold period is active. |
| **Claimable** | The hold period has passed. You can claim this earning from the dashboard. |
| **Claimed** | Paid out to your wallet (SOL) or bank account (USD). |
