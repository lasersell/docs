At LaserSell, our philosophy is simple: **We should only win when you win.**

Unlike traditional trading bots or swap platforms that charge a flat percentage on every single dollar you move (regardless of whether you lost money), LaserSell operates on a profit-first model. We specialize exclusively in the selling and closing of positions, ensuring our incentives are perfectly aligned with your success.

## 1. The Success Fee (5% of Profit Only)

This is our primary fee, and it applies **only when you leave a trade with more SOL than you started with.**

When you execute a profitable trade using LaserSell, we charge a **5% Success Fee** based strictly on your **Calculated Profit Estimate**.

- **How it works:** If you buy 10 SOL worth of a token and sell it for 15 SOL, your profit is 5 SOL. We charge 5% of that _profit_ (0.25 SOL).
- **The Difference:** Most competitors charge you based on the _total_ 15 SOL. We only charge based on the 5 SOL you gained.
- **Your Principal is Safe:** We never take a cut of your initial investment during a profitable trade.

## 2. The Safety Fee (0.7% Flat)

LaserSell provides advanced protection mechanisms, specifically **Stop Losses** and **Deadline Timeouts**, to protect your capital when a trade goes south.

If a trade is closed due to a Stop Loss or a Deadline Timeout (meaning the trade was likely break-even or a loss), the Success Fee is **waived**. Instead, a minimal **0.7% Safety Fee** is applied to the transaction value.

- **Why specific conditions?** This covers the infrastructure costs of monitoring your position while ensuring you don't pay high fees on trades that didn't go as planned.
- **Comparison:** This is significantly lower than the standard 1% fee most bots charge on _every_ loss.

## 3. Zero Buy Fees

Because LaserSell focuses exclusively on the art of the exit, **we charge 0% fees on your buy transactions.** You can buy using any platform you prefer, and rely on us solely for the execution of the sale.
