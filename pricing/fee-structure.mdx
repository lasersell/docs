A transparent breakdown of the costs associated with using the LaserSell platform.

LaserSell applies a **tiered routing fee** enforced directly on-chain for maximum speed and transparency. Your fee rate depends on your subscription tier.

## 1. The Routing Fee

| Tier | Fee Rate | Minimum Fee |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Personal (Free) | 2% (200 bps) | 0.01 SOL per trade |
| Tier 1 — Professional ($99/mo) | 0.9% (90 bps) | 0.01 SOL per trade |
| Tier 2 — Advanced ($249/mo) | 0.5% (50 bps) | None |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom |

- **Simple & Predictable:** No complex profit calculations and no hidden spreads. You pay the percentage listed above on the output volume, subject to the minimum fee for your tier.
- **On-Chain Enforcement:** The fee is handled atomically by the LaserSell Router on-chain program. It is deducted automatically from the SOL or USD1 proceeds during the transaction. USD1 transactions use the percentage fee only (no SOL minimum applies).

## 2. Network & Infrastructure Costs

To ensure your transactions land instantly there are two standard network costs involved. These are pass-through costs that go directly to the Solana network, not to us:

- **Jito Tip (Bribe):** A direct payment to validators to prioritize your transaction above others in the same block.
- **Solana Priority Fee:** A standard blockchain fee required to process high-priority computations on the network.
