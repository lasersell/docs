LaserSell is built to provide the fastest execution and most optimized routing across the Solana ecosystem.

## Supported Launchpads

- [Pump.fun](https://pump.fun)
- [Bonk.fun](https://bonk.fun)
- [Bags.fm](https://bags.fm)
- [Raydium LaunchLab](https://raydium.io/launchpad/)
- [Trends.fun](https://trends.fun)

## Supported Protocols

### [**Pump.fun**](http://Pump.fun) Ecosystem

- [**Pump.fun**](http://Pump.fun) **Bonding Curves**: Full support for buying and selling tokens directly on the [Pump.fun](http://Pump.fun) bonding curve.
- **PumpSwap:** Native support for trading on PumpSwap markets.

### **Raydium**

- **Raydium Launchpad (Non-Migrated):** Direct support for tokens currently on the Raydium launchpad.
- **Standard Pools / CPMM (Migrated):** Trade on standard Raydium liquidity pools for tokens that have migrated or launched directly to AMM.

### **Meteora**

- **Dynamic Bonding Curve / DBC (Non-Migrated):** Compatibility with Meteora’s DBC protocol for trading tokens during their initial bonding phase.
- **Dynamic AMM / DAMM V2 (Migrated):** Support for trading on Meteora’s dynamic automated market maker pools.

## Supported Assets

### **SOL (Solana)**

- **Native & Wrapped:** Full support for trading with native SOL and Wrapped SOL (wSOL).

### **SPL Tokens**

- **Universal Support:** Trade any SPL token listed on the supported platforms above.

### **USD1**

- **Stablecoin Support:** Specific support for trading pairs against the USD1 stablecoin.
