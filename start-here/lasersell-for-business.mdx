## Monetize Every Trade

Every transaction your users execute through LaserSell can include a [partner fee](/api/reference/partner-fees) routed directly to your wallet. You set the fee on each API request, either as a percentage (basis points) or a flat amount (lamports), and it is enforced atomically on-chain as part of the swap.

There is no invoicing, no reconciliation, and no off-chain payment flow. Revenue arrives in your wallet in the same transaction as the user's trade. You can verify every fee on-chain.

Partner fees are available on the Advanced and Enterprise tiers.

## Whitelabel LaserSell

Both business tiers include whitelabeling. Your users interact with your brand, your interface, and your product. LaserSell powers the exit engine and transaction building behind the scenes.

### Enterprise — Custom Pricing

Enterprise is for teams that have outgrown fixed limits or need guarantees a self-serve tier cannot provide.

**Custom capacity.** The Advanced tier caps at 200 wallets, 20,000 positions, and 1 session per API key. Enterprise removes these ceilings. If you are running a copy trading platform with thousands of followers, a Telegram bot with a large active user base, or an institutional desk managing many wallets simultaneously, Enterprise gives you the room.

**Custom routing fees.** At significant volume, the standard 0.5% fee is negotiable. Enterprise partners receive volume-based fee structures tailored to their throughput, including tiered discounts, monthly volume commitments, or flat fee arrangements.

**Dedicated namespace and SLA.** Enterprise accounts run on isolated infrastructure with guaranteed uptime SLAs. Your traffic is separated from other users and prioritized during high-load periods.

**Partner dashboards.** Real-time visibility into volume, fees collected, active sessions, and position metrics across all your API keys. Useful for finance teams tracking revenue and operations teams monitoring integration health.

**Custom monetization.** Beyond the standard partner fee system, Enterprise partners can work with us on custom on-chain revenue sharing. This includes multi-party splits, custom split configurations, and volume-based incentive structures. All enforced atomically on-chain.

**Dedicated support.** A private channel (Slack Connect, email, or Discord) with direct access to the engineering team. Priority response times for integration questions, incident escalation, and feature requests.
