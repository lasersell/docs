In high-volatility environments like [Pump.fun](http://Pump.fun), Meteora, and Raydium launches, the greatest threat to a trader's PnL is the **Execution Gap**. This is the physical and technical window between a market move happening and being able to react to it.

## The Human Reaction Limit

In the trenches, price action moves at a speed that makes manual trading structurally disadvantaged. When you trade manually, your execution relies on a multi-step chain:

1. **Perceive:** See the price move on a chart.
2. **Decide:** Determine whether to exit or hold.
3. **Act:** Move the mouse, select max, and click sell.
4. **Confirm:** Wait for the wallet popup and click confirm.

In a 2 to 5 second window, a **+100%** gain can become a **-50%** loss on low-liquidity moves. By the time a human completes the steps above, the opportunity has often already vanished.

## The Stale Data Problem

Most manual traders react to UI charts and trading interfaces. These interfaces must fetch, process, and render data before you see it. By the time the red or green candle appears on your screen, the market has often already moved or reversed.

LaserSell does not wait for the chart to catch up. It tracks moves in real time directly from the blockchain, acting on data as it hits the ledger rather than waiting for a visual representation.

## Layered Exit Strategies

Closing the execution gap is not just about speed. It is about having the right exit trigger fire at the right time. LaserSell evaluates multiple conditions simultaneously on every price update:

- **Take Profit:** Lock in gains the moment your target percentage is reached.
- **Stop Loss:** Cut losses automatically before they compound.
- **Trailing Stop:** Track peak profit and exit only when the price pulls back by a set percentage from the high. This lets winning trades run while still protecting gains on reversals.
- **Deadline Timeout:** Force an exit after a set number of seconds if no other condition has fired. A safety net for positions that go sideways.
- **Sell on Graduation:** Automatically exit when a token migrates from a bonding curve to a DEX (e.g. Pump.fun to PumpSwap), where price dynamics shift.

These strategies run together. Whichever condition triggers first executes the exit. No manual monitoring required.

## Optimized Transaction Delivery

Getting the exit signal right is only half the problem. The transaction still needs to land on-chain fast. LaserSell supports multiple transaction submission backends to minimize landing time:

- **Helius Sender** (default): Optimized transaction delivery through Helius.
- **Astralane**: Alternative submission service for users who want routing diversity.
- **Direct RPC**: Send transactions straight through your own RPC endpoint.
