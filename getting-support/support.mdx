title: "Support"
---

We are committed to helping you get the most out of LaserSell. If you encounter issues or have questions that aren't answered in the documentation, please reach out to us using one of the methods below.

**1. Open a Discord Ticket (Recommended)** For the fastest assistance, please join our official Discord server and navigate to the `#open-a-ticket` channel. This allows us to track your issue and coordinate with the team efficiently.

- **Server Link:** https://discord.lasersell.io

**2. Email Support** If you prefer email or have a sensitive inquiry regarding your license or billing, you can contact us directly.

- **Email:** [support@xen.world](mailto:support@xen.world)

**Response Times** Please note that our team operates on a "best effort" basis. While we aim to respond to all inquiries within **24 hours**, this response time is not guaranteed. We appreciate your patience as we work to resolve your issue.
