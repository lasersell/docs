If you encounter unexpected behavior, crashes, or have general questions about Lasersell, please reach out to us directly. We appreciate detailed reports as they help us maintain stability for all users.

### Contact Channels

- **Telegram:** [@xentzu](https://t.me/xentzu)
- **Instagram:** [@xen](https://instagram.com/xen)
- **Email:** `support@xen.world`

### Resolution Process

We aim to triage and fix confirmed bugs promptly.

- **Notification:** Once a fix is implemented, we will notify you immediately so you can pull the latest update.
- **Updates:** Check the [Releases](https://github.com/lasersell/apt/releases) to verify the fix is included in your build.
