The Analytics page gives you a data-driven view of your trading performance and a gamified achievement system to track milestones.

> **Note:** All analytics, insights, and achievements are based exclusively on trading activity done through LaserSell. Trades executed through other tools or directly on-chain are not included.

## Overview

Select a time period (24h, 7d, 30d, or All Time) to view your key metrics:

- **Total P&L** — Net profit or loss in your display currency
- **Total Volume** — Total trading volume in quote units
- **Win Rate** — Percentage of trades that closed in profit
- **Total Trades** — Number of closed positions
- **Avg P&L** — Average profit or loss per trade
- **Avg Hold Time** — Average time positions were open
- **Best Trade** — Your highest single-trade profit
- **Worst Trade** — Your deepest single-trade loss

### Daily PnL Chart

A bar chart showing your daily profit and loss over the last 90 days. Green bars represent profitable days, red bars represent losing days. Hover over any bar to see the exact figure.

### Exit Reasons Breakdown

A visual breakdown of what triggered your exits:

- **Target Profit** — Strategy hit your take-profit threshold
- **Trailing Stop** — Price pulled back from peak by your trailing percentage
- **Stop Loss** — Price hit your stop-loss threshold
- **Graduation** — Token migrated from bonding curve to DEX
- **Deadline** — Position timed out

This helps you understand which exit conditions are doing the most work and whether your strategy needs tuning.

## Insights (Tier 1+)

Personalized suggestions based on patterns in your recent trades. Each insight has a severity level (high, medium, low) and a specific recommendation.

Insights are generated server-side from your trade history and update periodically. They are gated to Professional tier and above.

If you are on the free tier, you will see an upgrade prompt.

## Achievements

Track your progress across **10 categories** with rarity tiers from Common to Legendary.

### Categories

| Category | What It Tracks |
| :--- | :--- |
| Volume | Trading volume milestones |
| Trades | Number of completed trades |
| Streak | Consecutive profitable trading days |
| Profit | Cumulative profit targets |
| Speed | Fast execution times |
| Single Trade | Single-trade PnL records |
| Win Rate | Win rate percentage milestones |
| Mirror | Copy trading milestones |
| Secret | Hidden achievements (discover them by trading) |
| Mastery | Advanced combination achievements |

### Rarity Tiers

- **Common** — Entry-level milestones
- **Uncommon** — Moderate accomplishments
- **Rare** — Significant achievements
- **Epic** — Exceptional trading milestones
- **Legendary** — The rarest achievements in the system

### Streaks

Your current and longest winning streaks are displayed at the top of the Achievements tab. A streak day is counted when you have at least one profitable trade routed through the LaserSell protocol.

### How Achievements Work

Achievements are checked hourly by the server. Only trades routed through the LaserSell protocol count toward achievement progress. When you unlock a new achievement, the app sends a notification.

<Note>
Unlocked achievements will be redeemable for rewards in the future. There is no LaserSell token and there will not be an airdrop.
</Note>
